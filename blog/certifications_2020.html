Top 3 Free Certifications for Digital Marketing


According to the EduCAT_XP team for the year 2020

3. Frictionless Sales, Hubspot Academy

A short sales-related course offered by Hubspot Academy with a focus on real-life situations. The certification which is offered after clearing the end-of-course exam is valid for a year.


2. Bing Ads Professional Certification, Microsoft

A free professional certification offered by Microsoft. The certification exam has no duration and can be taken as many times as required. You will be considered a Microsoft Advertising Certified Professional after clearing this certification exam.


1. Fundamentals of Digital Marketing, Google

An in-depth 40 hour digital marketing course (and certification exam) offered by Google Education in cooperation with The Open University, UK. The lessons are provided in easy to understand bites which don't exceed ten minutes. Additionally, this educational qualification is endorsed by IAB Europe (Interactive Advertising Bureau Europe A.I.S.B.L.).
